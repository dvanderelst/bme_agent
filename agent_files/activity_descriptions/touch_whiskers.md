Students are working on **touch and whisker sensing**. They have learned about human skin receptors (Merkel, Meissner, Pacinian, Ruffini) and completed a two-point discrimination activity to measure tactile acuity across body regions. They are now building a robot that uses flex sensors as mechanical whiskers for obstacle avoidance and wall following. Refer to the touch and whiskers documentation for activity details and troubleshooting guidance.
