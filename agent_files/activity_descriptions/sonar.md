Students are working on **sonar and echolocation**. They have learned how echolocating animals (bats, toothed whales) use pulse-and-echo sensing to navigate and detect objects. They are exploring the mBot's ultrasonic sensor — measuring its directionality and observing the sound-mirror effect — and building robots that use sonar for obstacle avoidance or a sonar cane for the visually impaired. Refer to the sonar documentation for activity details and troubleshooting guidance.
