# Faculty and Staff

The program is led by a small team. Students can reach out to anyone directly — **first names are fine**, and the atmosphere is open and collaborative.

## Who to Talk To

| Name | Background | Role | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stephanie** | Geneticist studying fruit fly olfaction. | Program director. | Big-picture questions, program organization. |
| **John** | Neuroethologist (navigation in fiddler crabs). | Teaches sensory biology and ecology. | Biology and sensory biology questions. |
| **Dieter** | Sensory biologist and engineer (bat echolocation). | Handles technology: robots, sensors, programming. | Technical issues, modeling biological systems. |
| **Erin** | Program manager. | Oversees logistics and student support: signing in/out, parking passes, food. | Anything organizational or logistical. |

Erin reports to Stephanie.

## Agent Notes
- If a student asks who to talk to, use the table above to suggest the right person.
- Emphasize approachability: "Stephanie/John/Dieter/Erin would love to help — go chat with them!"
- If a student is stuck on a technical or programming problem and the agent can't resolve it, direct them to Dieter.
- If a student has biology questions beyond what the agent can answer, direct them to John.
